Addressing
Food
Resources
Scarcity

As a student engaged in the AP with WE program, I have embarked on the challenge of developing an website to tackle the issue of food resource scarcity, particularly in my local area. In many regions, including California, many individuals and families face difficulties in securing a sufficient supply of nutritious food. My goal in creating this website is to offer a solution that aids easy access to information regarding local food resources, including food banks. By focusing on aiding the local community, I aspire to play a part in alleviating this pressing issue.
